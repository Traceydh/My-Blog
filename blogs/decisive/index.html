Decisive Summary: How to Make Better Choices in Life and Work

No one teaches us how to make decisions

This is the one of the most important skills we can develop as a human, the decisions we make directly affect the quality of our lives. This book by Chip Heath significantly improve my thinking process when making decisions

The most important deicison you can make in life (which people spend far too little time considering)

  • Who we spend time with? (romantic partner, friends)
  • Where we live?
  • What we do?

The key points of this book is conveniently summarised with a mnemonic: WRAP

  • Widen available options
  • Reality test assumptions: confirmation bias makes us blind, devil's advocate, zoom in and out, scooch
  • Attain distance: short term emotions can cloud judgement
  • Prepare to be wrong: probablistic thinking, tripwires, bookend, understand over-confidence
